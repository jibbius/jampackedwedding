About the Day

Part 1 : The Ceremony - 1pm

Please join us at the Rusty Gates in Curlewis for an outdoor, secular, ceremony.
The venue is a 20min drive from Geelong.
Feel free to bring partners and children.

The ceremony is expected to conclude finish at 3:30pm.
At the conclusion of the ceremony - afternoon tea and snacks will be served.

Part 2 : The Reception - 6pm

A reception will be held in the evening for a limited guest list (our closest friends and family).
The reception will consist of; a sit-down dinner, dancing, and more.

For friends that we were not able to invite to this portion of the day; we do appreciate their understanding.

Driving from Melbourne

The Ceremony venue is 20 minutes drive past Geelong (towards Port Arlington), and this may be the easiest way for you to get there.
Parking is available at both the Ceremony and the Reception.

Train from Melbourne (v/line)

Trains travel between Melbourne and Geelong roughly every hour, and you can pay with your MYKI.
From the station, you will need to take a taxi for the remainder of the journey to Curlewis (20 minute).
Taxis will generally already be waiting at the taxi rank outside the station.
